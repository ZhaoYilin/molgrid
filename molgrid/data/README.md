# Grid - Molecular Grid Generation Library

## 📚 Data References

### Lebedev Quadrature Grids
The Lebedev quadrature grids are based on the work of Vyacheslav Ivanovich Lebedev:

- Lebedev, V. I. (1975). "Values of the nodes and weights of quadrature formulas of Gauss‑Markov type for a sphere from the 9th to 17th order of accuracy that are symmetrical with respect to the center of the sphere". Russian Journal of Computational Mathematics and Mathematical Physics. 15 (1): 44–51.

- Lebedev, V. I. (1976). "Quadratures on a sphere". Russian Journal of Computational Mathematics and Mathematical Physics. 16 (2): 10–24.

- Lebedev, V. I. (1977). "Spherical quadrature formulas accurate to orders 25‑29". Siberian Mathematical Journal. 18 (1): 99–107.

- Lebedev, V. I.; Laikov, D. N. (1999). "A quadrature formula for the sphere of 131st algebraic order of accuracy". Russian Academy of Sciences Doklady Mathematics. 59 (3): 477–481.

### Atomic Radii Data (periodic_table.csv)

The atomic radii used in this library are compiled from the following sources:

- Slater, J. C. "Atomic Radii in Crystals". J. Chem. Phys. 1964, 41, 3199-3204. DOI:10.1063/1.1725697

- Cordero, B.; Gómez, V.; Platero-Prats, A. E.; Revés, M.; Echeverría, J.; Cremades, E.; Barragán, F.; Alvarez, S. "Covalent radii revisited". Dalton Trans. 2008, 2832-2838. DOI:10.1039/B801115J
